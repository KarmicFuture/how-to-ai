Example: AI automation for local businesses

Harbor HVAC — a 6-person heating & cooling company

Worked example · Local business

The situation

The owner misses evening quote requests while on jobs. By morning, two competitors have already called the homeowner back.

How it works (sample timeline)

  1. Monday — Free consult

    Walk through how leads arrive (web form, Google messages, voicemail). Count missed replies in the last 30 days.

  2. Tuesday — Scope the block

    Agree on one workflow: new inquiry → instant friendly text → owner alert → CRM note. Quote one 4-hour block (~$396).

  3. Wednesday–Thursday — Build

    Connect form/SMS tools, write AI reply templates in the owner’s voice, test with fake leads.

  4. Friday — Hand off

    Show a live demo. Leave a one-page “how it works.” Offer $150–$300/mo if they want weekly tweaks.

What you do

Set up the tools, write the message templates, test, and train the owner in under a week.

What they get

Leads answered in minutes instead of hours — without hiring a night receptionist.

How the money looks

First block ~$396. Two similar clients + light care ≈ $800–$1,200/mo.
