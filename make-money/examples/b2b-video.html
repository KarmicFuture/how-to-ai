Example: AI-assisted short video for locals

Bean & Branch Café — neighborhood coffee shop

Worked example · Local business

The situation

They post when the owner remembers. Followers like photos, but weekday traffic is flat. They want Reels without hiring a videographer.

How it works (sample timeline)

  1. Week 1 — Sample

    Film 20 minutes of phone footage (latte pour, morning line, pastry case). Deliver 2 sample clips free or cheap to win trust.

  2. Week 2 — Package

    Sell a monthly pack: 6 short videos + captions for $400/mo.

  3. Ongoing — Batch day

    One afternoon: AI scripts from weekly specials, edit, caption, schedule. Reuse the same 3 formats.

  4. Report

    Track saves, profile visits, and “was it busy after posting?” — not vanity likes alone.

What you do

Turn phone clips into a steady short-video habit with AI writing and editing help.

What they get

A reliable posting cadence that feels like their café — without living in CapCut every night.

How the money looks

One café at $400/mo; three locals ≈ $1,200/mo.
