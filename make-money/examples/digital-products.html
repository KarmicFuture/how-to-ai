Example: Small digital products for a niche

Product: “Airbnb Turnover Checklist for Local Cleaners”

Worked example · Local business

The situation

You already help cleaners with AI schedules. Ten of them asked for your checklist. That’s the product.

How it works (sample timeline)

  1. Ship

    Turn your checklist + message templates into a $19 PDF/Notion pack in one weekend.

  2. Page

    Simple checkout page. Three screenshots. Clear “who it’s for.”

  3. Sell

    Post in two local cleaner Facebook groups + email past clients.

  4. Iterate

    Add a $39 “pro” version from the questions buyers ask.

What you do

Productize something you already explain repeatedly.

What they get

A ready system instead of reinventing their turnover process.

How the money looks

20 sales = $380; a small email list can repeat that monthly.
