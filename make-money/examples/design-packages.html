Example: AI design packs for locals

Green Spoon — new juice bar opening downtown

Worked example · Local business

The situation

They need a menu board look, launch flyer, and a week of social graphics before opening day — budget is tight.

How it works (sample timeline)

  1. Pack sale

    Offer “Launch Kit”: menu layout + flyer + 8 social graphics for $350.

  2. Create

    AI concepts → you refine typography and brand colors → one revision round.

  3. Deliver

    Print-ready PDF + square/story sizes for Instagram.

  4. Upsell

    Monthly “fresh specials” pack for $150 if the launch goes well.

What you do

Ship a finished visual kit fast, without a big branding agency fee.

What they get

A cohesive look for opening week they can actually afford.

How the money looks

Two launch kits a month ≈ $700; add refresh retainers when ready.
