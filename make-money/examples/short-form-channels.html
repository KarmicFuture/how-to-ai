Example: Local niche short-form content

Your channel: “Home Fixes Under $100” for your metro area

Worked example · Local business

The situation

You’re not selling a client yet — you’re attracting homeowners, then offering a paid “room refresh consult” or affiliate tools.

How it works (sample timeline)

  1. Niche lock

    Pick one promise: quick, cheap, local home fixes. Film with a phone.

  2. Batch

    AI helps outline 20 hooks. Post 3×/week for 6 weeks.

  3. CTA

    Every video: “DM FIX for this week’s checklist” → email/SMS capture.

  4. Monetize

    Once DMs are steady, offer a $99 virtual consult or local partner referrals.

What you do

Build attention in a narrow niche, then attach a simple offer.

What they get

n/a (this path is often your own brand) — viewers get useful tips; you get leads.

How the money looks

Early months may be $0–$300; wins look like booked consults, not ad revenue.
