Example: AI social media for local shops

Nova Nails — busy nail salon

Worked example · Local business

The situation

Instagram is outdated. The owner takes great photos but never writes captions or posts on a schedule.

How it works (sample timeline)

  1. Audit

    Pick Instagram + Google posts. Agree on 10 posts/month for $300.

  2. Batch

    Owner drops 15 photos in a shared folder. You write captions with AI, edit for voice, schedule.

  3. Engage light

    Suggest 3 reply templates for common DMs (“prices?”, “walk-ins?”).

  4. Monthly review

    What drove bookings? Double down on that format next month.

What you do

Turn their photos into a month of posts without them living on their phone.

What they get

A filled calendar and a fresher profile that matches how good the salon actually looks.

How the money looks

Two salons + one boutique at $250–$350 each ≈ $750–$1,000/mo.
