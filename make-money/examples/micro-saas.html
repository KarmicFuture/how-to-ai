Example: Tiny paid tools for one job

Tool idea: “Review Reply Helper” for local clinics

Worked example · Local business

The situation

Dentists hate writing Google review replies. You start by doing it manually with AI, then wrap the workflow.

How it works (sample timeline)

  1. Manual first

    Serve 5 clinics at $99/mo: they forward reviews; you send reply drafts in 24 hours.

  2. Productize

    Build a simple form: paste review → get 3 on-brand replies. Charge $29/mo.

  3. Migrate

    Move happy manual clients onto the tool + a little human polish.

  4. Expand

    Add one feature they beg for (multi-location) only after retention is real.

What you do

Prove people pay for the job, then automate the boring middle.

What they get

Faster, kinder review replies without staring at a blank box.

How the money looks

10 clinics × $29 ≈ $290/mo; hybrid service+tool can reach $500/mo sooner.
