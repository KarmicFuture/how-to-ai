Example: FAQ & booking helpers

Bright Smiles Dental — small private practice

Worked example · Local business

The situation

The front desk answers the same questions all day: hours, insurance, “do you take new patients?”, parking.

How it works (sample timeline)

  1. Day 1 — Map FAQs

    List the top 10 questions from calls and the website contact form.

  2. Days 2–4 — Build

    Launch a site chat helper that answers those FAQs and offers “request an appointment” with name + phone.

  3. Day 5 — Soft launch

    Staff tests for a week. Fix wrong answers. Keep a human review of appointment requests.

  4. Month 2 — Care

    Optional $150/mo to update hours, insurance notes, and seasonal promos.

What you do

Build a narrow helper that answers the boring questions and captures booking intent.

What they get

Fewer repeat calls; after-hours visitors still leave a request instead of bouncing.

How the money looks

Setup $600–$1,000; care adds a small monthly if they want updates.
