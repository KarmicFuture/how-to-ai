Example: AI lead follow-up & reminders

ClearPath Moving — local movers

Worked example · Local business

The situation

Web quotes sit overnight. By the time someone calls back, the customer has booked another company.

How it works (sample timeline)

  1. Measure

    Pull last month’s form leads. How many got a same-day reply?

  2. Templates

    Write AI-assisted texts/emails in the dispatcher’s voice: thanks, next question, book a walkthrough.

  3. Wire it

    Form submit → instant reply → task for the human → reminder if no response in 24 hours.

  4. Prove it

    Compare response time and booked jobs for 30 days. Offer $150/mo to keep scripts fresh.

What you do

Make speed-to-lead automatic while humans still close the job.

What they get

Fewer cold leads; more conversations while the customer still cares.

How the money looks

Setup $400–$700; ongoing care optional.
