How to make money · Step by step

From first step to first payment

One shared template. Eight clear steps. Apply it to any of the ten income paths. Available for anyone to learn and use — AI is the lever; a clear offer is the business.

The professional template

Sell outcomes. Deliver with AI.

    Worked example

    Apply path one this week

    AI automation for local businesses — a strong fit for the free intro and $99/hour implementation blocks.

    1. Monday — Choose the outcome

      “Never miss a lead after hours” or “cut quote turnaround from 3 days to same day.”

    2. Tuesday — List 20 local buyers

      Contractors, clinics, salons, restaurants — anyone who lives on phone and inbox.

    3. Wednesday — Run 3 free intros

      Map their workflow. Leave a one-page note: problem, fix, hours, cost at $99/hr in 4-hour blocks.

    4. Thursday — Close a starter block

      2–5 hours to ship the first workflow. Only if monthly benefit clearly beats your fee.

    5. Friday — Deliver & ask

      Show the win. Ask for a referral and offer a light monthly care plan.

    Repeat the same week shape for video, chatbots, content, or social — only the offer changes. The template stays fixed.
