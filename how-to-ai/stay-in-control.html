Trust, privacy, and staying in control

AI should lighten your load — not become a second boss that you can’t question.

6 min read · For non-coders

Don’t paste what you wouldn’t put on a postcard

Skip customer private data, passwords, bank details, medical notes, and anything under an NDA. Use initials or fake names in examples. Share the shape of the problem, not the secrets.

Verify before you publish

Check prices, hours, legal claims, and medical or financial advice with a real source. AI can invent citations, statistics, and “facts” that sound polished.

Keep a human in the loop

  • You approve anything that goes to a customer
  • You decide the offer and the promise
  • You own mistakes — so review like you mean it

A simple weekly rule

If AI drafted it, you read it once for voice and once for truth. Two passes. Then send.
