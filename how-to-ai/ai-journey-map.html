The growth curve

Six stages on the AI paradigm

Every business sits somewhere on this path. The goal isn’t “more tech.” It’s freeing human energy for relationships and growth.

You are here
  1. Stage 1

    Pencil & paper

    Brick-and-mortar basics. Appointments, inventory, and notes live in notebooks, whiteboards, or memory.

  2. Stage 2

    Basic digital

    You have email, a simple website, or social pages — but work still jumps between apps and sticky notes.

  3. Stage 3

    AI curious

    Someone sometimes asks ChatGPT for a draft. Useful, but not yet part of how the business runs.

  4. Stage 4

    AI assisted

    AI regularly helps with posts, replies, and planning. Humans still copy-paste and push every button.

  5. Stage 5

    AI integrated

    Follow-up, FAQs, and content systems connect. Less retyping. Clearer handoffs. Time starts coming back.

  6. Stage 6

    Autonomous shop

    Routine operations largely run themselves. Your team’s best hours go to attracting and serving customers.
